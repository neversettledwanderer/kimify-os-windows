# Competitive Ads Extractor

This skill extracts your competitors' ads from ad libraries and analyzes what's working—the problems they're highlighting, use cases they're targeting, and copy/creative that's resonating.

## When to Use This Skill

- Researching competitor ad strategies
- Finding inspiration for your own ads
- Understanding market positioning
- Identifying successful ad patterns
- Analyzing messaging that works
- Discovering new use cases or pain points
- Planning ad campaigns with proven concepts

## What This Skill Does

1. **Extracts Ads**: Scrapes ads from Facebook Ad Library, LinkedIn, etc.
2. **Captures Screenshots**: Saves visual copies of all ads
3. **Analyzes Messaging**: Identifies problems, use cases, and value props
4. **Categorizes Ads**: Groups by theme, audience, or format
5. **Identifies Patterns**: Finds common successful approaches
6. **Provides Insights**: Explains why certain ads likely perform well

## Instructions

When a user requests competitive ad analysis:

1. **Identify Target Competitors**
   - Clarify which competitors to analyze
   - Confirm platforms (Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, etc.)
   - Set date range (current active ads vs. historical)

2. **Extract Ad Data**
   - Navigate to platform ad libraries
   - Search for competitor name or brand
   - Filter by active/inactive, date range, region
   - Capture screenshots and text for each ad
   - Record: headline, body copy, CTA, creative type, landing page

3. **Analyze Messaging**

   For each ad, identify:
   - **Pain Point**: What problem is highlighted?
   - **Use Case**: What scenario is depicted?
   - **Value Prop**: What benefit is promised?
   - **Target Audience**: Who is this speaking to?
   - **CTA**: What action is requested?
   - **Creative Approach**: Image, video, carousel, etc.

4. **Categorize and Pattern-Match**

   Group ads by:
   - Theme/message (e.g., "ease of use", "cost savings", "security")
   - Audience segment (e.g., "enterprise", "small business", "developers")
   - Funnel stage (awareness, consideration, conversion)
   - Creative format (static, video, carousel, testimonial)

5. **Synthesize Insights**

   Deliver analysis as:
   ```markdown
   # Competitive Ad Analysis: [Competitor Name]
   
   ## Overview
   - Total ads analyzed: [X]
   - Active campaigns: [X]
   - Primary platforms: [list]
   - Date range: [dates]
   
   ## Messaging Themes
   
   ### 1. [Theme Name] ([X] ads)
   **What they're saying**: [Summary]
   **Why it works**: [Analysis]
   **Example ad**: [Screenshot reference + copy]
   
   ### 2. [Theme Name] ([X] ads)
   ...
   
   ## Target Audience Breakdown
   | Audience | Ad Count | Key Messages |
   |----------|----------|--------------|
   | [Segment] | [X] | [Messages] |
   
   ## Creative Patterns
   - [Pattern 1 with examples]
   - [Pattern 2 with examples]
   
   ## Opportunities for Your Brand
   1. [Gap or angle competitors aren't using]
   2. [Messaging that could be differentiated]
   3. [Audience segment being underserved]
   
   ## Ad Swipe File
   [Organized screenshots with annotations]
   ```

## Tips

- Focus on active ads for current strategy insights
- Compare messaging across platforms (B2B on LinkedIn vs. B2C on Facebook)
- Track changes over time to spot strategy shifts
- Save screenshots in an organized folder for future reference
- Look for indirect competitors targeting the same audience
- Note seasonal patterns or campaign-based bursts
